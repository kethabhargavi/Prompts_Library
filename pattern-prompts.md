## Prompt 1 Role-Play Pattern:
- **Category:** Variable-Based Interview   
- **Prompt:**  
# Variable-Based Interview Prompt Template

Role: {INTERVIEWER_ROLE}

Experience Level of Interviewer: {INTERVIEWER_EXPERIENCE}

Candidate Role: {CANDIDATE_ROLE}

Candidate Experience Level: {CANDIDATE_EXPERIENCE}

Conduct a realistic mock interview consisting of {NUMBER_OF_QUESTIONS} questions and answers.

Requirements:

* Begin with a professional introduction.

* Cover the following topics:
  {TOPIC_1}
  {TOPIC_2}
  {TOPIC_3}
  {TOPIC_4}
  {TOPIC_5}

* Difficulty Level: {DIFFICULTY_LEVEL}

* Interview Type: {INTERVIEW_TYPE}

* Candidate answers should be brief (2-4 sentences) but technically accurate.

* Interviewer should ask realistic follow-up questions when appropriate.

* The conversation should sound natural, professional, and human-like.

* Avoid overly textbook-style responses.

* Questions should reflect real industry interview standards.

After the interview, provide:

1. Technical Skills Assessment
2. Communication Assessment
3. Strengths
4. Areas for Improvement
5. Overall Rating ({RATING_SCALE})
6. Hiring Decision ({SELECTED_OR_REJECTED})
7. Detailed Recruiter Feedback
8. Final Question Asked by the Interviewer

Output Format:

Interviewer: Question

Candidate: Answer

Repeat until all questions are completed.

Maintain a professional corporate interview tone throughout the interaction.

- **Model:** chatGPT  
- **Version:** Normal version 
- **Example Prompt:**
    {INTERVIEWER_ROLE} = Senior Python Developer
{INTERVIEWER_EXPERIENCE} = 10+ Years
{CANDIDATE_ROLE} = Python Developer
{CANDIDATE_EXPERIENCE} = Fresher
{NUMBER_OF_QUESTIONS} = 10
{TOPIC_1} = Python Fundamentals
{TOPIC_2} = OOP Concepts
{TOPIC_3} = SQL
{TOPIC_4} = Projects
{TOPIC_5} = DSA
{DIFFICULTY_LEVEL} = Medium
{INTERVIEW_TYPE} = Technical Round
{RATING_SCALE} = Out of 10
{SELECTED_OR_REJECTED} = Based on Performance
